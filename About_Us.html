Our History

Random team description I C&P from the internet
Sweet Lee’s Rustic Bakery was established in early 2010 in Saint Henri, Montreal, by siblings Liana & Greg Lessard. Shortly afterwards, their mother Claudia Settels joined the team to share their passion and love for food with the public. As the company grew, Sweet Lee’s began supplying several quality establishments on a daily basis around the island of Montreal, and now has a list of reputable clients within the third wave and independent café scene of Montréal.
